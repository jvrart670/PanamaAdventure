Food

Background

There is a lot to eat in Panama and the food can be a great experience and these are some of the most popular dishes in Panama: Sancocho: Traditional chicken soup made with chicken, ñame (root vegetables) and culantro (recaito). Carimañolas: A dough made of yuca that is stuffed with ground beef and until golden brown. Hojaldra: is a fried bread. Panamanian ceviche: fish or seafood marinated in lime juice, chiles, onions, salt and pepper it is usually eaten with crackers. Bollos: They are a sweet corn dough that is boiled in a corn husk.

TDuring the Christmas and New Year Panamanians will gather with friends and family and celebrate by eating dishes such as: Tamales, Pernil (roast pork), Ham, Turkey, Rice with Pigeon Peas, Rosca de Huevo(bread), Chicha de Saril(Hibiscus drink), Eggnog with Rum and Caribbean Fruit Cake.
